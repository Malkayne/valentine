Our Story

We met on a rainy Tuesday afternoon at a cozy little coffee shop downtown. Alex was reading a book by the window, and Jordan accidentally spilled coffee while reaching for a napkin. What started as an embarrassing moment turned into hours of conversation, laughter, and an undeniable connection.

From that day forward, we've been inseparable. Our relationship has grown through countless adventures, late-night conversations, and supporting each other through every challenge. Every moment together feels like a blessing, and every day we fall more in love.

This Valentine's Day, we celebrate not just our love, but the beautiful journey that brought us together and the exciting future that awaits us.
